The concept behind this event is to encourage participants to revisit games that have encountered one of two common problems, as identified by TA (TrueAchievements):

  • Server Closure
  • Achievements listed as Unobtainable/Discontinued/Partially Discontinued
Gasp, shock, awe! But fear not, completionists! There will be numerous ways to participate and earn bonus points without having to confront the dreaded unobtainables directly.

The easiest (although least likely) way to participate would be to go back and mop up games that you may have already earned all of the discontinued (or grabbed all of the online achievements before the server shutdown!). If you don't have any of those on your tag - there are a number of games that have had online servers shut down, but do not have any online achievements - resulting in them still being able to be fully completed. Games like Army of Two, Dead Space 2, and I Am Alive.

The real kicker, and the main thought process behind this event, is that this month we're going to also allow games that are no longer able to be completed to count for bonus points. As with previous months - bonus points are going to be awarded as a multiplier of the games base BCMX value. This multiplier will be the same across the board for any game that qualifies for the bonus. The multiplier will be +80% of a game's BCMX score.

There will, however, be a cap to the bonus points available for each user this month. A cap of 3,000 bonus points will be available - if this threshold is reached no more bonus will be earned for the duration of the month. This was done as a precaution due to this bonus being an extremely experimental bonus.

Important Rule: For any completion to count towards this bonus the ratio must be at or above 1.5 and have a time estimate at or above 5-6 hours. Or the time estimate must be at or above 20 hours regardless of ratio. There is no ruling against stacks. Any game released in April of 2024 will not qualify for this bonus. Additionally, any completion that is due to a new DLC or TU releasing in April of 2024 also will not qualify for this bonus.

But wait, there's more!

Continuing with the trends from previous months - we'll be offering a Community Bonus to strive for inside of this bonus!

We're going to switch things up this month. And instead of offering a blanket bonus worth X points - we're going to challenge you lovely gamers to build the bonus points pool up. For each game that is played that qualifies for the above bonus, 5 points will be added to a pool. The pool will max out at 1,000 bonus points. Each instance of a game counts - so if three people played Mirror's Edge, it would equate 15 points rather than 5.
