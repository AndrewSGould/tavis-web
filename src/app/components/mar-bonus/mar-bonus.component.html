The concept of the Bounty Board, however, is that the more gamers that complete a particular game - the less bonus points that particular game ends up being worth! You'll end up having to split the bounty with others, so to speak. BCM veterans should be familiar with this bonus as a whole, but we're going to (of course) be trying out a new scoring mechanic this time around!

Similar to February, a multiplier will be applied to the bounties base BCM value as additional contestants complete the game.
  • 1 Gamer Completed = +400% of BCM Value as bonus points
  • 2 Gamers Completed = +300% of BCM Value as bonus points
  • 3 Gamers Completed = +200% of BCM Value as bonus points
  • 4 Gamers Completed = +100% of BCM Value as bonus points
  • 5 Gamers Completed = +95% of BCM Value as bonus points
  • 6 Gamers Completed = +90% of BCM Value as bonus points
  • 7 Gamers Completed = +85% of BCM Value as bonus points
  • 8 Gamers Completed = +80% of BCM Value as bonus points
  • 9 Gamers Completed = +75% of BCM Value as bonus points
  • 10 Gamers Completed = +70% of BCM Value as bonus points
  • 11+ Gamers Completed = +65% of BCM Value as bonus points

Continuing with the trends from previous months - we'll be offering a Community Bonus to strive for inside of this bonus!
