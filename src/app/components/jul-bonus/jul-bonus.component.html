Tamriel Tea Party

To celebrate that fateful day on the 16th of Evening Star, AD 1773 we've decided to have you all re-enact one of the most renowned protests of Tamrielic history. Yes, we're talking about the day that some brave khajit's threw their wares overboard and sparked a revolution!

The Tamriel Tea Party will have an incredibly simple premise! At it's core - we're asking you to complete games with at least one letter "T" in the title. Easy, right? Now, to make matters slightly more constraining - games will need to have equal to or less than 1,773 completions at the final update of the month! And then, of course, to make it even more constraining - only ID@Xbox games will count! We've got to celebrate Indie'pendence, right?

The bonus points system will work as follows:

  • <= 1,773 completions = 40% of BCMX value + (# of T's in the title % of BCMX Value)
  • <= 500 completions = 60% of BCMX value + (# of T's in the title % of BCMX Value)
  • <= 100 completions = 80% of BCMX value + (# of T's in the title % of BCMX Value)

As a practical example - The Elder Scrolls Online is worth 1,500 Base BCMX points. With only 14 completions (at the time of writing this blog) that would place it in the highest bonus tier of 80%. Additionally, there is a single T in the title so it would receive an additional % (totaling 81%). The total bonus points for this game would then be 1,215 (in addition to the base score).

To get participation credit for this event you will simply need to complete one qualifying game.

But wait, there’s more!

We are adding a community-focused goal to this event! As all good citizens of Tamriel know, those brave khajit dumped exactly 342 crates of tea into the Padomaic Ocean.

The goal, as a community, is to complete 342 games that qualify for this event. So, 342 ID@Xbox games with less than or equal to 1,773 completions at the end of the month and have at least a single T in the title.

Everyone’s completions will contribute towards this goal. The completions do not have to be unique games, the same game completed by 3 different people will count as 3/342. If met, everyone who qualifies will receive 1,000 bonus points.

To qualify for the community bonus you will need to complete any number of qualifying games that have a total of at least 5 T's in the title. Yes, if you can do this with a single game - that will work.

This is an all-or-nothing bonus pool.

  • For any completion to count towards this bonus the ratio must be at or above 1.5 and have a time estimate at or above 5-6 hours. Or the time estimate must be at or above 20 hours regardless of ratio.
  • Stacks will count as separate games due to technical restrictions.
  • Any game released in July of 2024 will not qualify for this bonus. Additionally, any completion that is due to a new DLC or TU releasing in July of 2024 also will not qualify for this bonus.
  • Games must be ID@Xbox according to the categories on TA
  • Games must have 1,773 completions or less at the end of the month
  • Games must have at least one T in the title
