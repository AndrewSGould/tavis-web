To gain participation credit for the month - you simply have to earn bonus points by completing any qualifying game. Please don't forget the standard game restrictions listed at the end of the blog.

We will be using Gamerscore as the metric to determine game eligibility. Unfortunately, this will result in some games that should qualify, like Kameo or Like a Dragon Infinite Wealth not qualifying because they still have base 1,000 GS despite receiving DLC.

It will also result in some games that technically shouldn't qualify, like Rare Replay, as actually qualifying due to having over base 1,000 GS despite not having updates.

But wait, there’s more!

The goal, as a community, is to complete any number of qualifying games that have a total of 40,000 achievements (or more).

Almost everyone’s completions will contribute towards this goal. The completions do not have to be unique games, the same game completed by 3 different people then the achievement count will be multiplied by 3 for the tally. If met, everyone who qualifies will receive 1,000 bonus points.

To qualify for the community bonus you simply need to earn bonus points in this event.

This is an all-or-nothing bonus pool.

Important Rules:

  • For any completion to count towards this bonus the ratio must be at or above 1.5 and have a time estimate at or above 5-6 hours. Or the time estimate must be at or above 20 hours regardless of ratio.
  • Stacks will count as separate games due to technical restrictions.
  • Any game released in August of 2024 will not qualify for this bonus. Additionally, any completion that is due to a new DLC or TU releasing in August of 2024 also will not qualify for this bonus.
  • Games must have over 1,000 total GS.
