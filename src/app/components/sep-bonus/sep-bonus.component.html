It's September - which means it's time for the annual September Streaking event! This marks the sixth iteration of it - so a good portion of you should be familiar with the overall concept by now!

You'll be tasked with building a streak using various traits based on your game completions. These traits will be based on criteria available in various Game Collection columns. What are the traits this time around, you ask?

  • Shared Developer: The publisher field must list the same publisher. Slight variations will not count. For example - Ubisoft Paris is not equal to Ubisoft Montreal.
  • Shared Word The title of the game must have a shared word of at least 3 letters in length. Platform tags will not count. Yes, "the" counts.
  • Shared Release Year: The year of the release must match. We've had global releases for quite some time now, but in the case of discrepancies we will be using US release dates. I don't foresee this causing any problems, but I'm outlining it to be safe.

You cannot use the same trait twice in a row. Meaning, you can't link 3 games in a rows from the same release year, etc. You must alternate traits. You do not have to cycle all traits, but can bounce back and forth between the same two if desired.

Since this is going to be automated - the very first valid streak that is picked up will be your start. You also will not be able to manually overlook any games in your streak. Be sure to pay attention to your completion order!

To gain participation in this bonus you will need to complete a streak of 3 or more games.

The standard BCMX game restrictions apply.

Scoring will be determined as follows

  • Streak of 3+ = +40% of BCMX value to all games in streak
  • Streak of 5+ = +60% of BCMX value to all games in streak
  • Streak of 7+ = +80% of BCMX value to all games in streak

The community goal this time around has two criteria that must be met:

  • There must be a total of 40+ streakers (40+ gamers have a valid streak)
  • There must be a total of 150+ games inside of the streaks

If met, all contestants who had a valid streak will earn 1,000 bonus points. This is an all or nothing bonus.

Important Rules:

  • For any completion to count towards this bonus the ratio must be at or above 1.5 and have a time estimate at or above 5-6 hours. Or the time estimate must be at or above 20 hours regardless of ratio.
  • Stacks will count as separate games due to technical restrictions.
  • Any game released in September of 2024 will not qualify for this bonus. Additionally, any completion that is due to a new DLC or TU releasing in September of 2024 also will not qualify for this bonus.
